## üìÑ 5. Business Model (English)

### Business Philosophy

Deutsch.vn is built on a model of **free education access**, combined with a powerful toolset for teachers, schools, and learners who want to go further.

It does not sell classes or conduct teaching itself ‚Äì instead, it builds **infrastructure and tools that support education**.

---

### Business Structure

#### 1. **Free for all learners (B2C ‚Äì Free):**
- Entire learning library is open to everyone.
- No login required, no ads.
- Core learning experience is fully free.

#### 2. **Optional premium tools for learners (B2C ‚Äì Optional Tools):**
- Learners can choose to use advanced tools:
  - **Readflo** ‚Äì contextual reading and word tracking.
  - **AI writing coach** ‚Äì auto scoring and feedback.
  - **Listening trainer** ‚Äì real voice with personalized difficulty.
- Freemium model ‚Äì free core, pay-for-more optionally.

#### 3. **Platform tools for schools & teachers (B2B ‚Äì SaaS model):**
- Offered services:
  - Private Moodle plugin to post lessons and exercises.
  - Shared database (cloud) for managing learning content.
  - Learner tracking and performance analytics.
- Monthly subscription-based.

---

### Revenue Overview

| Customer Type      | Product / Service                        | Model         |
|--------------------|-------------------------------------------|---------------|
| Learners (B2C)     | Premium tools (Readflo, AI tools)         | Optional paid |
| Schools/Teachers   | Moodle plugins, content management tools  | Subscription  |

---

### Core Values

- **No class selling ‚Äì purely support-focused.**  
- **Tools first ‚Äì not profit first.**  
- **Community-focused and neutral by design.**
